The Quackening

You are a duck on an island in middle of nowhere and evil creatures want to invade your island and destroy your home. Start off with the main tower, fend off invaders by spawning ducks that attacks them – using strategic planning, advanced warfare tactics.

A 3D tower-defence game revolving around ducks and wonky physics. This game was mode for a studio-module in a team of 7. I was the programming lead of the team, in charge of implementing the core mechanics such as the UI and tower placements, drag and drop and enemy attack systems using Unity C#. I was also involved in the designing and quality checking of the game.

Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Role: Programming Lead
Grade: A

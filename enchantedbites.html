Enchanted Bites

Enchanted Bites is a 2D action-adventure mobile game developed by Cooked Studios. You are a witch who opened up a tavern. Fight monsters, gather ingredients and discover new dishes to sell to your customers!

As the programming lead, I was in charge of developing some of the game’s key features. This includes the UI, inventory, item, dialogue and cooking system, making sure they are as modular as possible using tools such as Unity’s scriptable objects system. I was also entrusted with the optimisation of the game and ensuring the game worked as intended. Below are screenshots of the features I developed for the game.

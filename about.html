About me

Hey! My name is Jayson. I have been exposed to game development ever since I was 11 and began creating my own projects at 14. I have worked on 20+ games, many of which are solo passion projects as well as some group projects where we worked on bigger games. Ever since, my passion for learning, making games and making a difference has only been growing stronger.

I pursued a Diploma in Media, Arts and Design (Animation and Games) from 2023 to 2026, where I was exposed to many industry practices and worked with people who shared the same rigor on making games. Feel free to check out my games and projects here!
